our profile

who are we

Vel electronics is an electronics product manufacturing industry that currently running in Venkateswarapuram, Tirunelveli district. It has highly educated professionals for management & research and well trained & experienced employees for manufacturing & service.

We strictly follow on dignity and quality in each and every aspect of our company. Our company slogan says two things, that’s

  • Developing the electricity from renewable & sustainable energy resources
  • Reducing the loss of power in electricity by advanced embedded controls
The major part of our product in particular, comparative performance is based on sustainable green energies and advanced technologies that reduce the loss of power. Currently we are manufacturing LED bulbs, Emergency lights, Embedded controls for street light and motor pump sets and Charger for rechargeable battery items. We also providing solar power systems for Home, Shop and industry. The management would try to carry out this company beyond the industry average with global competitive environment.
